Funding amount:

$5,000

Team
Theresa Webb
Jenny Wilson
Description

Grant includes work on Funding Rates page, Rewards & Tracking Simulator, and CLI Trading tool. All will be combined in a community accessible webpage that will continue to host additional work done and maintenance on behalf of Chaos Labs.

Purpose

Funding Rates page will help promote and guide traders around Perpetual premiums found across all public traded on dYdX. Rewards & Tracking simulator will help market the benefits of trading on dYdX and assist current traders in mapping out their rewards. The CLI trading tool will make it easier for non-UI based traders to execute quickly on dYdX. The general purpose of this grant is to recruit Chaos Labs, a very qualified contributor, to work with dYdX on current and future projects.

Execution

3 months to completion. Each individual grant is estimated to take 120 days, but will be worked on concurrently.

Payment structure

25% upfront / 75% at completion of all Grants
